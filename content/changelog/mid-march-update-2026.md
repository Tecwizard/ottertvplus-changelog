### Back to buisness as usual...

The UPS has finally had nearly all it's batteries replaced.

I'll admit, it wasn't cheap at all. $400 for 20 12V 9A SLA Batteries and the system still needs 8 more which are currently priced at $160. But I have finally put the UPS back into operation and will replace the last 8 batteries at a future date.

Of course I'm not gonna repeat my last mistake and opted to leave the remaining battery module empty this time instead of leaving the old batteries in there.

I have also added temperature sensors to all 5 UPS modules so that I can monitor battery temps and get an alert if a battery bank starts overheating. This was all done using temperature probes and an ESP32 connected to Home Assistant with ESPHome. I might post a guide somewhere but we'll see.

No new media to report at this time but I do plan on adding Zootopia 2 once I get my hands on the Blu-ray. Until then, it's back to our regularly scheduled programming.
